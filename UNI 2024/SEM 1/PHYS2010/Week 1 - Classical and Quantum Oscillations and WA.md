$$\frac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial t^{2}} = c^{2} \nabla u$$


**Schrodinger's Equation**
$$i \hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi (r,t) = \left[  -\frac{\hbar^{2}{2 \mu}}+V(r,t_{}) \right]$$
